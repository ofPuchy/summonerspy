About Summoner Spy

Summoner Spy was created on an idea for a school project. The idea of looking up matches and summoner data goes back further than the idea of Summoner Spy. This site is inspired mainly by op.gg since this is one of the largest websites which provides these functions.

Since the riot api had the data, why not share it with the entire League of Legends community? This is how Summoner Spy was born. It's simple but provides a lot of information about a summoners rank and his main champions.
